B. Tech for working professionals in Mechanical and automobile engineering

Mechanical and automobile engineering course is specially designed to impart the all-around theoretical and practical knowledge of different fields that are covered in an automobile. It is a branch of engineering that deals with the design, manufacturing and operations of automobiles. This course is the study of automobiles to building them from scratch. Mechanical and automobile engineering is one of the best career options for individuals who are interested in fast-revving cars and want to learn everything about it.

Why study B. Tech for Working professionals in Civil engineering?

Automobiles have made the world fast and moving. Students who are willing to upgrade their careers via higher studies in Mechanical and automobile engineering can pursue this course. It is a professional engineering course that consists of the study of Research and Development, Design, Development, testing, manufacturing and operation of a vehicle.

Well paid jobs

From small bikes to heavy trucks, everything comes in automobile engineering. The aspirants of Mechanical and automobile engineering are more likely to get a well-paid jobs in top companies. Also, there is a huge scope for career growth in the field of Mechanical and automobile engineering. Aspirants with creativity and good experience in the field are bound to grow faster. Candidates from this course achieve great compensation for their service. B. Tech in mechanical and automobile engineering can get a good starting salary according to business norms.

Challenging works

The life of mechanical and automobile engineers is filled with new challenges every day. The motive and work of the engineer are to find a new solution to the problems. The course helps the aspirants to learn new skills and execute them whenever required. Candidates who are likely to get challenged regularly naturally get excited about mechanical engineering.

Course subjects

B. Tech in mechanical and automobiles Engineering course consists of various subjects which enlightens you with the all the required knowledge. This course helps you in gaining both theoretical knowledge and industry exposure. Candidates will study various subjects to meet the industry requirement. Here are some lists of topics during the course.

  • Automobile Chassis and Transmission
  • Applied Thermodynamics and Heat Transfer
  • Mechanics of Machines
  • Automotive Fuels and Lubricants
  • Engine Design
  • Robotics
  • Automotive Aero-dynamics
  • Chassis Design
  • Robotics
  • Vehicle Maintenance
  • Engine and Vehicle Management System
